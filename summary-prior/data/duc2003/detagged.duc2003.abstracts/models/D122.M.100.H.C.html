The two distinct activities of the cashew business , growing and harvesting cashew nuts and then processing and exporting them , constitute import sources of jobs and revenue for emerging nations . After steadily increasing production acreage , in 1997 Vietnam exported 33,000 tons of processed cashews to become the world 's second largest exporter . Cold , drought , destructive insects , technology , skill of growers and government policy could all affect production . Mozambican cashew nut processors , saying they would have to close because local growers were exporting the raw nut , appealed to the government for protection . India and Sri Lank formed a joint venture to help increase their nut exports .
