Increased exposure to ultraviolet light has been linked to an increase in cataracts , skin cancer and can suppress the body=92s immune system . Increased exposure to UV light is also harmful to plant and animal life . Scientists are seeking ways to protect the ozone layer which filters out the damaging UV rays and are advocating the phasing out of CFCs by 2000 . CFCs are known to deplete the ozone layer . To reduce the exposure to UV , people are being advised to wear sunglasses and to wear protective clothing and hats . Extra care should be taken with children , whose eyes are especially sensitive to UV radiation .
