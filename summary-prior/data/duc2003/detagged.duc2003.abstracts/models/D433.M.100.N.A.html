Stoicism , the quiet acceptance of pain and defeat , dates from an ancient Greek school of philosophers including Epictetus . Hindus practiced stoicism in medieval times and perhaps on to this day in facing the onslaught of Islam . It is identified as one of `` the great Victorian virtues . '' A recent ballet , `` Stoics '' , is described as exposing `` the sheer bloody awkwardness of life ... '' and in 1992 a vice presidential candidate introduced Epictetus and the stoics into the political debate . As dreary as it may seem , stoicism is still with us after all these centuries . Perhaps we will just have to put up with it .
