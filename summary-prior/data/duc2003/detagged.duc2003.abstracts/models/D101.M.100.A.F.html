Flooding on the Yangtze River in 1998 , the most severe since 1954 , left thousands dead , millions homeless , and threatened over two million hectares of farmland . Over a million soldiers and civilians were pressed into service fighting the floodwaters . In many cases , dams and dikes were destroyed in an attempt to disperse water upstream and lessen the impact downstream . Abnormal movement of the rain front lingering along the mid and lower Yangtze coupled with torrential rains in the upper Yangtze brought about the devastating flooding . Excessive logging on hillsides , now banned , contributed since the eroded soil left behind could not absorb much water .
