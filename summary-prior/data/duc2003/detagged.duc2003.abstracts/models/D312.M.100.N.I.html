Hydroponics , the process of growing plants in nutrient-rich water instead of soil , allows for strict controls , often with computers , of water and nutrients and lends itself to indoor gardening . Indoors , light can be controlled to simulate the normal growing seasons and allow harvests throughout the year . Marijuana is frequently grown illegally under these conditions . Even carbon dioxide can be added to force plants to mature at low heights . Utah State University is growing dwarf wheat in a hydroponics experiment aimed at feeding space colonists in the future . Chicory is being grown in total darkness , and some people have hydroponics tomato patches .
