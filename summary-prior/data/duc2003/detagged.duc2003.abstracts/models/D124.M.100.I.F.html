Medecins Sans Frontieres ( MSF ) , known in the US as Doctors Without Borders , was formed in 1971 , by a group of French doctors frustrated while serving with various international humanitarian organizations . The doctors found that these organizations were inefficient and reluctant to denounce those responsible for the problems requiring medical assistance . MSF adheres to the fundamental principle `` that all disaster victims have a right to professional assistance given as quickly and as efficiently as possible '' . MSF volunteers generally work in harsh conditions . The organization is quick to denounce hostile elements or governments whose actions aggravate these conditions . In 1999 , the MSF was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize .
