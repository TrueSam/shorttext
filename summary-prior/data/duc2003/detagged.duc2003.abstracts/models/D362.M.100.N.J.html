During an INS crackdown on alien smuggling operations that netted 79 illegals at the Atlanta airport in February 1989 , officials learned that some people paid up to $ 4,000 for air fare , meals , housing , and a U.S. job . There is also evidence of forced confinement of illegal aliens in `` drop houses '' until they or their relatives pay a ransom . In June 1989 , federal officials found 47 immigrants involuntarily held in a `` drop house . '' In August 1990 , 90 Latin American illegal immigrants were found similarly detained . In one raid of a `` drop house , '' a fee schedule for the services the immigrants required was seized .
