Anorexia nervosa is an eating disorder in which patients starve themselves . Bulimia is an eating disorder in which patients binge on food and use laxatives or self-induced vomiting to keep down their weight . Causes of these disorders include focusing a child on cleaning his plate ; trying to avoid foods prohibited in childhood ; trying to remain thin ; poor self-perception ; and for women , struggling in a male-dominated society . An addiction to opiates produced by the brain while dieting may cause anorexia nervosa . Starving can lead to stuffing and binge eating , as found among bulimia victims . Treatments for these eating disorders include always monitoring eating habits and taking Prozac .
