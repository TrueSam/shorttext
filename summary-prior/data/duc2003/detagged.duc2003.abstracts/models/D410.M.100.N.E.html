The Schengen Accord , named for the town in Luxembourg where it was signed , is designed to abolish border controls and speed up cross border traffic . The accord is scheduled to go into effect in early 1994 but Great Britain , Ireland , and Denmark are resisting abolition of all border controls . Great Britain , for example , wants to ensure that unwanted non-EC travelers do not slip through the EC passport lane at ports and airports . The Schengen Accord does commit member countries to abolish internal checks , but only when sufficient measures , such as computer links between Schengen immigration authorities , have been put into place .
