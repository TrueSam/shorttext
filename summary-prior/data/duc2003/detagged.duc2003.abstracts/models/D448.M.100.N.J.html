At least three ships sank in 1989 due to severe weather conditions . In February , a Dutch chemical tanker was lost in gales in the Algerian port of Skikda . In October , a Saudi Arabian taker was abandoned after it caught fire during a storm in the Persian Gulf . In December , the Romanian freighter Tololovem sank in heavy seas off Spain 's northwest coast . The fate of a fourth ship , a Norwegian freighter , the Stokksund , is uncertain . Four nations searched for it in early February after it disappeared in gale force winds across the North Sea . Some crewmen were reported missing in all four incidents .
