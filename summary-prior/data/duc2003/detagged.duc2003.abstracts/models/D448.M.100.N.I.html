Weather is the scourge of ships , from the loss of a ship in Drakes Bay in 1595 to the sinking of the ferry Estonia in the Baltic in the 1990 's . Hurricanes destroyed a steamer in 1915 and a Greek freighter in 1990 . A typhoon sunk a bulk carrier off the Japanese coast . Ships off the coast of Spain fell victim to high seas and fog . The stormy North Atlantic has taken its toll with rough weather and gale-force winds . The Endurance was trapped by ice and crushed in 1915 . Ships were also sunk by storms in the Persian Gulf and off the coast of New Zealand .
