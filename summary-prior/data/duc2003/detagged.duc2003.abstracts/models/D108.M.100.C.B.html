Iowa 's precinct caucus was held on January 24 , followed by New Hampshire 's first-in-the-nation primary on February 1 . By March 2000 , the Republicans had staged primaries or caucuses in 32 states . By then , most of the party 's convention delegates had been apportioned . Prior to the fourth GOP contest in South Carolina on February 19 , seven Republican contenders were forced to drop out . Steve Forbes followed , and with Alan Keyes as the only marginal candidate still standing , the George Bush-John McCain showdown was set . After Bush won sweeping victories on Super Tuesday , March 7 , McCain halted his challenge , leaving Bush as the presumptive Republican nominee .
