A 1997 documentary film challenged the FBI~Rs claim that only non-burning grenades were used during the 1993 raid on the Branch Davidian compound . Fire destroyed the compound . Eighty cult members died . In 1999 the FBI admitted using military CS gas that used an incendiary device to disperse the gas . Congress was in a furor . FBI Director Freeh asked for an independent investigator . Former Senator Danforth was named . Danforth 's preliminary report , released in July 2000 , said pyrotechnic devices were used only against a concrete bunker , and absolved FBI agents of guilt in any Davidian deaths . There was no major cover-up , but the FBI was criticized for lack of candor .
