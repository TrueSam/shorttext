In 1987 , the British vehicle ferry Herald of Free Enterprise capsized off Zeebrugge with the loss of 193 lives . The bow doors had not been closed properly . In September 1994 , more than 900 people died when the vehicle ferry Estonia sank in the Baltic Sea . Its roll-on , roll-off design caused it to capsize completely in a few minutes . Several months after the sinking of the Estonia , a Philippine ferry sank with a loss of more than 140 lives . In April 1991 , an Italian car ferry , the Moby Prince , ran into an oil tanker outside the port of Livorno and 140 people died in the blaze .
