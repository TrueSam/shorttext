In the United States , where laws have regulated child labor for many years , efforts focus on enforcement . Tactics used include nation-wide crackdowns , sting operations , raids , surprise inspections , mandatory jail terms and fines , suits and injunctions , and publicizing violators . China passed the first national child labor law in 1991 . Some provinces also passed laws and began enforcement campaigns . Raids were conducted in Thailand . In India , where law enforcement is lax , monetary grants were being tried to rescue individuals sold into forced labor . Some businesses have begun to insist that suppliers and sub-contractors ban child labor . International efforts focus on boycotts of goods .
