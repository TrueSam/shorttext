Charles W. Schulz , the creator of the `` Peanuts '' cartoon , was born in Minnesota in 1922 . He wanted to be a cartoonist as a child . After graduation from high school , he took an art course but was drafted into the Army where he served during WWI . After the war , he began selling cartoon to various newspapers and magazines . In 1950 , seven newspapers bought his `` Peanuts '' strip and by 1955 , he won the Reuben outstanding cartoonist award . By 1999 , `` SPeanuts '' characters adorned 20,000 different products . He died of colon cancer shortly after retirement from nearly 50 years of drawing every `` Peanuts '' strip .
