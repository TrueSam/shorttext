The Three Gorges Project in China 's Yangtze River will take 17 years and cost an estimated 50.09 billion Yuan . It is the world 's largest and most complex hydroelectric and water conservancy project and is expected to boost China 's economy . European countries are investing $ 1.1 billion . Experts from throughout the world are consulting on the complex technology . More than one million people must be moved . Some of them will be resettled to coastal areas , bringing subsidies to local governments . Even plants are being moved , most into a newly established park . A mobile communications network has already been established for the area .
