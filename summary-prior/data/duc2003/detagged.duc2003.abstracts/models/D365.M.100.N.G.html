The temperature warming effect of El Niino on weather patterns causes excessive or erratic rainfall , floods , severe winter storms , and droughts . These developments diminish food stocks , the growth of crops essential to world trade ; e.g. , cocao , fish , soybeans , This , in turn , leads to lowering of commidity prices , higher interest rates , and lower GDP growth . El Nino also causes thousands of deaths and billions of dollars in damage globally and even severely disrupts habitats of endangered species . The coincidence of El Nino with other climatogical events ; for example , volcanic eruptions , lunar and solar cycles , and greenhouses effects , can cause more extensive effects .
