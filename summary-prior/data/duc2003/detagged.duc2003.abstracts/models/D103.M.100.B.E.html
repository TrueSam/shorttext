Despite charges of fraud , eBay and other web site auctions on the internet continue to grow . eBay downplays the amount of fraud in internet auctions and guarantees purchases up to $ 200.00 . Still , there have been questions raised concerning the authenticity of one-of-a-kind sports memorabilia being offered and some sellers have been successfully prosecuted . eBay , founded in 1995 , has provided the model for others to follow as it continues to expand its offerings ; most recently offering used cars in an agreement with AutoTrader.com . While safety appears to be a minor issue , the greatest threat to eBay and others is outages , which can affect the profits of buyer and seller alike .
