In February-March 2000 the normally desert nation of Mozambique experienced devastating rains which caused the worst floods in 30 years . At least 492 people were killed and close to a million were left homeless . The floods wiped out crop fields creating an extended food shortage . The grave situation was exacerbated by land mines moved by the floods to new unknown locations . International relief organizations , the U.N. , and national governments rushed financial and material aid to the stricken nation , but some deemed it too little too late . In May 2000 the international community pledged $ 452.9 million for reconstruction of the Mozambique infrastructure .
