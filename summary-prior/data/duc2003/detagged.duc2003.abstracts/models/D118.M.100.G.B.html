Over 30,000 people were killed in black-on-black violence and hundreds of thousands were displaced during the anti-apartheid struggle in South Africa . In Kenya 's Rift Valley Province , where the Kikuyu and Kalenjin tribes are clashing , over 120 were killed and hundreds brutally injured in less than one month . Kenyan President Daniel arap Moi is a member of the Kalenjin tribe . The ongoing Crisis is Burundi has led to the deaths of more than 150,000 people since 1993 . The Central African Republic , one of the poorest in Africa , was shaken by several mutinies in 1996 and is troubled by tribal tension .
