Veteran commercial pilots take an annual 12-hour re-training course where emergency flight situations are simulated . The USAF recently commissioned private schools to perform their introductory flight training . The Moody Aviation School trains missionaries who graduate as pilots who double as flight engineers . Boeing organizes flight seminars on flight safety for Chinese Civil Aviation . The Chinese Air Force trains many female pilots at its Changchun Flying Academy . Most of the students at the large Mojave Test Pilot School are foreigners . It was closed temporarily in January 2000 because it lacked proper licensing . The U.S. Navy motivates college students by providing a week-long aviation tryout .
