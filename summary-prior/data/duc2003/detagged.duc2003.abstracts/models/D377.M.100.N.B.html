The popularity of cigar smoking is increasing . Business at Cigar Warehouse was up by about 30 % over the previous year . Cubatabaco increased its output and almost doubled its six-size range of cigars . With a social climate in which the cigar is tolerated , smokers ' rooms are being established and `` smoker '' events are being offered more often . Ritz-Carlton hotels havei smoking dens . At the Four Seasons Hotel in Newport Beach , cigar smoking is permitted in a dining room . At the Chez Cary restaurant in Orange , cigars are available on request . At the Ma Maison Sofitel Hotel in West Hollywood , Cigar Smokers ' Nights are held once a month .
