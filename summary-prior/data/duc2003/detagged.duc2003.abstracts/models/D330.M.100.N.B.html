Since the 1980-1988 Iran-Iraq war , the governments of Iran and Iraqi have sought to normalize relations . They 've exchanged thousands of prisoners . Iraq withdrew from Iranian territory it had held since the war . In exchange for Iranian aid , Iraq delivered Iranian moujahedeen to Iran . The countries gradually agreed to upgrade their diplomatic representation to ambassadorial level . They 've agreed to share control of the Shatt al- ' Arab waterway and are mutually determined to prevent the establishment of a separate Kurdish entity . They 're discussing joint policy against Washington , and embarking on joint economic action . A project to export Iraqi oil through an Iranian province is under study .
