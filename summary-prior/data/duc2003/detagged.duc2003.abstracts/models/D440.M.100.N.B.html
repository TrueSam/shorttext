Although the U . S. Labor Department has strict child-labor laws , ironclad enforcement has not been achieved . Enforcement policies include surprise inspections , court injunctions , fines , and publication of names of violators . Trade preferences can be withdrawn from countries that abuse worker rights and there are moves in Congress to ban all imports of goods made by children . Enforcement of child-labor laws in third-world countries is highly problematic . International organizations have applied pressure . The ILO and ICFTU have launched global campaigns to eradicate child labor . Levi Strauss , of denim fame , and the Swiss retailer Migros insist that suppliers and sub-contractors ban child labor .
