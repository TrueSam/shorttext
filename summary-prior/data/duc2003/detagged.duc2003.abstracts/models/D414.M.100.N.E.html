Cuba exports in excess of 6 million tons of sugar in a year . The largest recipient of sugar is Russia , which receives over half of the total amount exported by Cuba . China receives about 740,000 tons of sugar while Japan and Canada each import about 500,000 tons each . Other recipients of Cuban sugar include , Italy , Iran , and South Korea . In most cases , Cuba exports sugar in exchange for goods received . For example , Cuba received nearly 10m tons of oil from Russia in exchange for sugar . China supplied food and medicines while Italy supplied semi-finished food products in exchange for sugar .
