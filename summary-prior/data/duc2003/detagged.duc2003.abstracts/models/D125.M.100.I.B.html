Neutrinos are subatomic particles that carry no electrical charge , and are so light that they were assumed for many years to have no mass at all . Three types of neutrinos are known to exist . Neutrinos rarely interact with other matter , but scientists successfully designed an experiment to catch a glimpse of sparks created when neutrinos collide with atoms . To avoid distortion , the Super Kamiokande international research team worked in an abandoned mine shaft in central Japan . They detected that some neutrinos oscillate , or change type , as they pass through space or matter . According to widely accepted theory , if something oscillates , it must have mass .
