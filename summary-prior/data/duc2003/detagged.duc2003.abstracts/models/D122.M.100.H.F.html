The demand for cashews had led several third world countries to increase production . Vietnam , now trails only the world 's leaders , India and Brazil . Cashew trees like any agricultural product are subject to the fluctuation of climate and of soil condition . Also they are subject to the policies of the government and the country 's special interests and to external influences such as the World Bank . Mozambique cashews grow on small farms of poor farmers . They are processed under a state monopoly that arbitrarily sets low prices and employs many workers at low wages . The World Bank provides financial assistance and advice to Mozambique to improve production but also puts conditions on its loans .
