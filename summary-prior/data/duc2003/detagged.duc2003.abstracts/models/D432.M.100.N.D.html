Two incidents in California exemplify the controversy that surrounds what is , or what appears to be , racial profiling . A Hispanic motorist contended that his arrest in the San Diego area was made because of his color , and not because he illegally passed another vehicle . A Long Beach police officer arrested and manhandled a black man who was wearing dirty clothes and driving an old car . The black man , a participant in a videotaped `` sting '' operation , claimed that he was the victim of discrimination . Customs officials along the United States-Mexican border have established profiles of smugglers that have led to arrests .
