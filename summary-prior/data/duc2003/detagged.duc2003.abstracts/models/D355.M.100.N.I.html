Since the United States began a spaceborne ocean remote sensing program in 1978 , several other nations have launched such programs and many otheri nations began receiving and analyzing the digital data from other nations ' satellites . Orbiting sensors include Synthetic Aperture Radar ; multi-spectral electric self-spanning , visible and thermal infrared , and multi-channel microwave scanning radiometers ; and ocean color monitors . Information gathered includes water temperature ; currents ; mud , sand and chlorophyll concentrations ; red tide occurrences ; and other contaminations . Data are used to predict worldwide weather ; support the fishing , ocean transport , and petroleum industries ; track environmental changes ; and create general models of the ocean 's atmosphere system .
