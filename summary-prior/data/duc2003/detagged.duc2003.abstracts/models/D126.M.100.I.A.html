The creation of Canada 's Inuit territory of Nunavut , April 1 , 1999 , was a victory for the patient , peaceful aboriginal advocates of self-governance , but the celebration of the birth of the new territory of 770,000 square miles and 27,000 population was tempered by grim socioeconomic facts of life . Rates of suicide , unemployment , teen-age pregnancy and substance abuse were sky high . Crowded housing contributed to a birth rate higher than Mexico 's . In the first year of Nunavut 's life the number of sexual assaults were ten times the Canadian average . The future held the threat that Nunavut would become a perpetual welfare state .
