Despite a large worldwide market for cashew nuts , certain regional problems often hamper productivity . Lack of capital and raw materials , destructive insets , cold weather , and no direct access to processing companies deters Vietnam from reaching its high potential . Tanzanian production declined due to disease , insects , low prices , ageing trees , and lack of expertise . Mozambique industry suffered from lack of raw materials due to a system of exporting without processing . Vietnam 's productivity is hampered by a prolonged drought . Sri Lanka 's unsatisfactory yield may be resolved by mutual collaboration with India . Meanwhile , World Bank programs are resolving certain problems .
