Charles Schultz , who died from colon cancer at 77 on the eve of the publication of his last comic strip , touched a world-wide audience . His Peanuts comic strip began publication in seven newspapers on 2 October 1950 . Gaining in popularity , it ultimately came to be published in 75 countries in 2,600 newspapers . Peanuts spawned an entire industry which included books , articles of clothing , television programs , and Broadway plays . The characters in Peanuts , unlike other comic strips , appealed to a cross generational audience , appealing to adults and children alike . The foibles of Charlie Brown , Snoopy , Lucy , and others spoke to readers for almost 50 years , touching the hearts of millions .
