NATO has decided to invite the Czech Republic , Hungary and Poland to join its alliance in April 1999 . The three countries agreed to strengthen their mutual political , economic , military , and cultural ties in anticipation of benefits from NATO membership . Canada enthusiastically supports Czech entry and promises to expand its investments there . France also promised its support and Britain praised the Czech progress toward membership but offered an ambitious program for much needed improvement of the Czech army . The Czech PM criticized Russia 's ambassador for saying Russia would reconsider its economic relations if the Czechs persisted in trying to join NATO .
