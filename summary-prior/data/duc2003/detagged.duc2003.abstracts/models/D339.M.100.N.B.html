Recently developed Alzheimer 's drugs such as piracetam , oxiracetam , tacrine , and physostigmine work to inhibit an enzyme that breaks down acetylcholine . Tacrine ( Cognex ) is the first drug specifically approved in the US for the treatment of Alzheimer 's . It eases symptoms , but does n't alter the underlying dementia process.i Deprenyl citrate is claimed to delay the effects of Alzheimer 's . Thirty to forty percent of patients may benefit from THA , but at best can expect only temporary improvement . None of a new class of memory enhancing drugs called nootropics , even Aviva , can do more than temporarily reverse or slow down mental degeneration .
