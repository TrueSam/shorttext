In the early 1990s , despite the anti-smoking campaigns , there were a few cigar enthusiasts who promoted the enjoyment of a good cigar . A hotelier in the Los Angeles area established a smoking room where cigar smoking was welcomed and encouraged . The Four Seasons Hotel in Newport Beach permitted cigar smoking in the Pavilion dining room . Some women in Boston started their own cigar club , and Anwar Sadat 's daughter was a member . New cigar smokers emerged , and some tobacconists said they were serving younger customers who bought the higher priced cigars . Cuba and the Dominican Republic have increased their cigar productions .
