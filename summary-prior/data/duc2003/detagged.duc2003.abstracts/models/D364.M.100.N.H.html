Rabies has been confirmed in Latin America , the Middle East , Europe , Asia , and the US. Recently , 60,000 Chinese died of rabies within six years . Corrective action has included animal vaccination ; restriction of slaughtered cattle transfer , or animal trading or exhibition ; listing and marking of pets and keeping them locked in houses ; and destroying , or revaccinating and quarantining infected animals . In Shanghai , all pet dogs were ordered killed after an outbreak . Bats , jackals , foxes , and wild dogs have been eliminated , by marksmen and poison . Vaccinated bait has prevented rabies ' spread among wildlife . Improved monitoring , reporting , law enforcement , laboratory capabilities , international cooperation , and availability of vaccine supplies has been urged .
