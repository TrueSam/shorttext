0n 12 January 1990 , a North Hollywood woman was sentenced to a year in jail and three year 's probation for drunk driving that killed a Sherman Oaks woman in 1989 . At the time of the accident , the driver 's blood-alcohol level was 0.1 % , then the legal standard for intoxication . Since then the standard has been lowered to .08 % . Municipal Judge John Hunter , during the June 1990 trial of a Ventura woman accused of killing three men while driving with a blood-alcohol level of .20 % , opined that the higher a driver 's blood-alcohol level , the higher the probability that an accident will result in death .
