In the last few years , scientists around the world were seeking to refine our knowledge of schizophrenia . Abnormalities in two specific chromosomes were linked . Thalamus deficiencies were discovered . Dopamine pathways and relationships with opiate pathways were being examined . Smaller olfactory bulbs were detected . Schizophrenics were more likely to have been born in the winter and in an urban area , and have more creases on their hands . Perhaps , they were delivered by caesarian section . Early diagnosis and treatment was an issue . Magnetic stimulation to the brain was being tried . Even with all the knowledge , known treatments were not being used as well as they should .
