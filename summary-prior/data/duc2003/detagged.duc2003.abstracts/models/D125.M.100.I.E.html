Neutrinos , sub-atomic particles whose existence was postulated long before their existence was confirmed , have been called Nature 's most elusive substance . First predicted in the 1930s , the existence was first confirmed in 1951 when Dr. Frederick Reines and Dr. Clyde Cowan captured traces of their passing . Study of the elusive particles continued from an observatory in Antarctica and from an abandoned mine in Japan . The Japanese site , called the Super-Kamiokande detector , is located inside a mountain one mile underground . Studying traces left by neutrinos passing through a cathedral-sized tank of water , scientists were able to confirm that neutrinos have mass .
