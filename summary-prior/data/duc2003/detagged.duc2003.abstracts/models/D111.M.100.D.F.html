Cell phone use by drivers is believed by many to be dangerous and should be outlawed . Others believe that it is no more dangerous than other distractions confronting drivers . Many jurisdictions have considered bans on cell phone use while driving . Some of those jurisdictions have limited the use of cell phones but outright bans are few , despite strong campaigning by victims or near victims of talking drivers . Some European countries have banned the use of cell phones while driving . The number of cell phones in use is increasing each year and phone companies strongly oppose any restrictions and cite the phones value in emergencies .
