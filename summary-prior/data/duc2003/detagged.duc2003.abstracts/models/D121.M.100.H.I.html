The International Criminal Police Organization ( Interpol ) was created in 1923 . Headquartered in France , its 177 members try to work together fighting crimes , holding frequent conferences , and sharing information and techniques . Crimes that cross borders , including drug trafficking and smuggling , have long been targets . Car thefts and smuggling along with insurance fraud have mushroomed in Europe with the end of the Cold War . Europe is also concerned about sex crimes -- child pornography , pedophile tourism , prostitution rings , and human smuggling . As criminals become more sophisticated , cooperation is being urged on terrorism , money laundering , and , most recently , cyberspace crimes .
