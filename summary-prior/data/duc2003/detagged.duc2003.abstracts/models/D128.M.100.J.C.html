Mozambican flooding , the worst in 30 years , began in mid-February , 2000 and continued , almost unabated , until mid-March . First the southern and central parts of the country were hit , then the north . Almost 500 people died and one million were either homeless or in desperate need of help . 250,000 people were sheltered in 64 camp care-centers . Foreign countries provided early rescue with planes , ships and helicopters to pluck people from trees and rooftops and to ferry in foodstuff and medical aid . Eventually , the United Nations made a U.S. $ 450 million appeal from its community of nations for flood relief and rebuilding .
