Several important undertakings have been associated with the construction of Three Gorges Dam . The construction of cofferdams began after the damming of the Yangtze in November 1997 . Also in 1997 , the first power transmission line , between Changshou County and Wanxian , was installed . By November 1999 , 190,000 residents of the area had been resettled . Housing for an additional 80,000 had been prepared . Over one million people and 1600 companies and factories are expected to be affected by the relocation project . In August 2000 , GSM base stations were installed in the Three Gorges area to provide telecommunications support to the construction activities .
