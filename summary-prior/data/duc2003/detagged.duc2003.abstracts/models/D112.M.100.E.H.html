The Hubble Space Telescope , launched in 1990 , was designed for at least four service calls by visiting shuttle astronauts to repair and upgrade equipment . The first , in 1993 , installed new optics to correct an immediately apparent mirror problem . The second occurred in 1997 . The third was scheduled for June 2000 , but deteriorating gyroscopes threatening observation capability prompted emergency repairs . A planned October 1999 mission to replace the gyroscopes was delayed by shuttle problems until December , then shortened to avoid Y2K complications . A second service mission was scheduled for May 2001 . A final servicing mission was planned for 2003 , although NASA since decided to operate Hubble through 2010 instead of 2005 .
