Millions of cars have been recalled for a variety of problems . The most common reason was problems with the emission system with eight recalls . Malfunctioning seat belts , airbags , and seats followed with a total of five . Two recalls were for leaks in the fuel line . One recall concerned a wiring problem that caused 34 fires . There were eight other recalls for a variety of reasons . Ford recalled the most cars , 3.65 million ; General Motors recalled 3 million ; and Chrysler recalled 606,000 . Japanese manufactures recalled 473,000 , Hyundai recalled 28,000 , Rover recalled 50,000 , and Volkswagen recalled 650,000 .
