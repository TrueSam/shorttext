Hydroponic farming is a method of growing plants that is totally different for the traditional soil method . It involves the use of a nutrient rich water base and controlled grow lighting . The promise of this method is that more food can be produced on a smaller surface area . This would have immediate application to providing fresh food in areas where space is at a premium and it could also serve to support humans during space travel . Drug dealers have been quick to seize upon this method by growing marijuana in basements or other secluded interior areas .
