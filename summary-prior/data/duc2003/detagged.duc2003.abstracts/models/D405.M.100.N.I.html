From 1987 to the mid-1990s , scientists detected significant cosmic phenomena and massive structures in the universe . Ripples of matter -- fluctuations in the after-glow from the Big Bang -- were observed . A supernova , the birth of two quasars , an eclipsing millisecond pulsar with its white dwarf companion , and two asteroids were identified . Scientists began getting a look at the black hole in the Milky Way Galaxy by identifying giant `` blobs '' surrounding it . A string of galaxies , dubbed the Great Wall , is 500X200X15 million light years in size . One galaxy stretches for 6 millions light years . Knowledge of our Sun 's atmosphere also was greatly expanded .
