Polls show that a clear majority of Americans support the death penalty . Thirty-eight states have adopted it . During the last few years , at least 11 states considered bills to halt or abolish capital punishment , and four states rejected bills that would have established it . Issues such as incompetent defense lawyers , misconduct by prosecutors , and denial of crucial DNA testing have touched off wide debates over the fairness and justness of capital punishment . Some states have declared moratoriums on the death penalty . Leaders of religious denominations take varying stands . The Supreme Court is preparing four significant rulings on capital punishment .
