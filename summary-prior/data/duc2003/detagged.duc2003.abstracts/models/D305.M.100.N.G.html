According to an insurance group study of the rates of car crashes , consumers have a better chance of survival in a larger vehicle . Of the ten vehicles with the lowest rates , three were large , six were mid-size , and only one was small . Topping the list of 1985-87 models with the ten lowest driver/passenger fatalities were the Volvo 740-780 , four door ( 0.6 ) and the Ford Taurus station wagon ( 0l7 ) . Of the highest death rates , the list was led by the Chevrolet Corvette ( 5.2 ) and the Chevrolet Camaro ( 4.9 ) . The General Motors pick-up trucks with side-mounted fuel tanks are being recalled because of high fatality rates .
