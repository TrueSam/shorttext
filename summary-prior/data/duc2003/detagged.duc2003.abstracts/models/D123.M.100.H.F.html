Pilot training is conducted in a variety of ways throughout the world . Several commercial schools exist in the United States and many cater to foreign pilots-to-be . Some are narrowly focused , such as the Moody Missionary school which trains pilots to support missionary work . China has increased pilot training overall and has increased the number of woman pilots in the military and in civil aviation . Specialized training exists to teach pilots new techniques and to cope with unforeseen situations . Military air forces train their pilots but some , the US for example , are beginning to use commercial schools . Concerns arose in the US because of pilot training of foreign students , but these concerns were set aside .
