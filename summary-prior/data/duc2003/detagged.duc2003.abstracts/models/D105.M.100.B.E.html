Swiss Olympics official Marc Hodler accused the Salt Lake City Olympics Committee of bribery in its bid to host the 2002 Winter Olympics . Salt Lake lost its 1991 bid for the 1998 Winter Games to Ngano , Japan , but won the 1995 bid for the 2002 games by a landslide . Under investigation is the role a $ 400,000 scholarship fund played in achieving that result . The money went to at least 13 people , including relatives of IOC members . In addition , money also went to pay for the mayoral campaign of an Ecuadoran official . Salt Lake officials denied any wrongdoing and claims the money was for humanitarian purposes .
