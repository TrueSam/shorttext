Nine members of the European Community : Germany , France , Italy , Spain , Portugal , Greece , and the Benelux Countries , have agreed to implement the Schengen Accord . Britain , Ireland , and Denmark are reluctant to go along with that agreement . The Schengen Accord , named for the city in Luxembourg where it was signed , will allow for free access by EU members across internal borders . Border security arrangements are yet to be worked out for non EU travelers and some Benelux Airports are being given extra time to implement procedures . The agreement also provides a framework for commonality of immigration and criminal records access .
