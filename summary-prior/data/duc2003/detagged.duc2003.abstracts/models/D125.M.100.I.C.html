Neutrinos are , ghostly sub-atomic particles that travel at close to the speed of light , have no electrical charge , and have minimal interaction with other matter . They were hypothesized in 1931 but not discovered experimentally until 1956 . First thought to be without mass , the discovery of their mass in 1998 affects long-standing fundamental theories of how particles work . This has profound consequences about the formation and evolution of matter , the galaxies and the ultimate fate of the universe . They may explain the universe 's `` missing mass '' . Existing in three types , called `` flavors '' , the third and smallest was not discovered until year 2000 .
