Y2K , the risk of computer failure due to year 2000 non-acceptance , could affect both large computers and 150 million microchips , causing power outages ; telecommunication and transportation problems ; food , water and gas shortages ; and security problems . Health-care device malfunction could endanger patients or disrupt care . Financial problems could result from stock exchange or cash register malfunction , lack of Y2K insurance coverage , or lawsuits against non-compliant businesses . Small and medium-sized businesses would be most endangered . `` Y2K problem two '' involves problems stemming from media-stoked fears , talk of Y2K disasters , rants about God punishing man for reliance on technology , and last-minute panic . Acting on fears , people could alter investments , buying and behavior patterns .
