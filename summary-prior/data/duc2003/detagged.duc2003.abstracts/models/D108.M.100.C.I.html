The 2000 Republican presidential candidate was known after Super Tuesday . The field narrowed from 11 in mid-1999 , to four by February , to Bush alone . The various methods states use to select delegates were examined and manipulated , and changes for 2004 were discussed . States vied to select first . Super Tuesday , with 16 states , left the Western Primary irrelevant . California tried to end winner-take-all for 2000 , then its blanket primary was declared unconstitutional . After the governor of New York failed to restrict the 2000 election , he sought to widen the 2004 . Some politicians want to lengthen the process and counteract the power of specific small states and regions .
