Interpol 's president echoed calls today to strengthen and modernize Interpol to combat increasingly sophisticated organized crime . International cooperation is essential and several examples of such cooperation are underway . In Asia , China is cooperating with neighboring countries to combat the organized drug trade while the European union has begun to work with neighboring countries to combat drug trafficking , organized crime and child pornography . Interpol also accepted an Egyptian proposal for the international community to share information to combat terrorism . Interpol also , in a first ever move , has hired a private consulting firm to create a network to combat cyber crime .
