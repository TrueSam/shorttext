White House intern , Monica Lewinsky , revealed in a taped conversation with a friend that she had an affair with President Clinton . She was reprieved from testifying in the Paula Jones case in which she might have had to declare why she and the President denied the affair . Later ordered to testify before grand jurors , she was granted full immunity in exchange for testimony . The House released written evidence of Lewinsky 's account of their affair . Early the next year , the Senate agreed to review and release her videotaped deposition . After a 20/20 apearance , a critic judged her sincere but shallow .
