Research indicates that an addiction to opiates produced by the brain during dieting may be the cause of anorexia nervosa . Anorexia nervosa is an eating disorder which causes people to starve themselves , sometimes to death . The opiates in question are called endorphins . Endorphins normally cause a high which increases hunger . In anorexics , however , the hunger is overridden by euphoria . Starving also leads to binge eating , a trait common also to individuals with bulimia . Bulimia involves individuals who see themselves as overweight or who think they will become overweight . Bulimics typically binge when eating and then make themselves vomit to remain thin .
