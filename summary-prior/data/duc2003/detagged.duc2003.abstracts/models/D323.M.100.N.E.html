Although plagiarism was common in earlier periods , Virgil , for example , borrowed owed from Homer , it was n't until Shakespeare 's time that writers began to complain about it . Coleridge and Laurence Sterne both were accused of plagiarism . More recently , accusations of plagiarism have been leveled against Senator Joseph Biden and Marten Luther King . The charge against Biden , that he plagiarized a speech by Britain 's Labor Party leader , Neil Kinnock , forced Biden to withdraw from the Democratic presidential primary race . Allegations of plagiarism against King arose as scholars , assembling his papers for publication , found passages in his dissertation that were not credited or iadequately credited to their sources .
