The English novelist , Martin Amis , complained that the novel , `` Wild Oats '' , by Jacob Epstein contained 53 distinctive passages almost identical to those in his own `` Rachel Papers . '' Jacques Attali was accused of , and vehemently denied , plagiarism in his book `` Verbatim '' about French President Mitterand . Stanford University said that Dr. Martin Luther King , Jr. had plagiarized , or inadequately credited other authors in his doctoral dissertation . Senator Joseph Biden of Delaware was accused , and later exonerated , of plagiarism while at Syracuse University . In another type of plagiarism , humorist Art Buchwald sued Paramount Pictures , contending that the movie `` Coming to America '' was based on a published idea of his .
