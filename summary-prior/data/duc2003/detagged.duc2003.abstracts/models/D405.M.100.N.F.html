US satellites in space have discovered previously unknown phenomena in the vast reaches of outer space . A so-called `` lumpiness '' in the universe has been noted and is believed that gravity can form planets from this lumpiness . `` Blobs '' , which are clouds of hot charged gas particles exist around Sagittarius A , each measuring a trillion miles in diameter . A narrow sheet of galaxies half a billion light years in diameter , constitute one of the largest `` structures '' in the universe . The satellites have also found supernovas ( exploding stars ) , neutron stars ( pulsars =AD remnants of exploding suns ) and evidence of the possibility of a black hole in the center of the Milky Way .
