Of 25 fatal accidents that were not resolved as to cause , two occurred when the driver headed the wrong way on a divided freeway . Four occurred when the driver went off the right side of the road , one missing a curve along a winding highway and plunging down a cliff . Twelve involved a driver veering or drifting into oncoming traffic , with two cars crossing a median . Seven occurred when the driver lost control . Four of these incidents happened on a freeway , with two cars hitting the center divider . One out-of-control car crashed into a restaurant . At least five of the accidents occurred after midnight .
