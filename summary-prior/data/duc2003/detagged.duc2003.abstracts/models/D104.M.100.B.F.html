Persistent questions regarding FBI actions during the siege of the Branch Davidian compound near Waco and of a possible cover up have led both Attorney General Reno and FBI Director Freeh to pledge new investigations . Suspicions were heightened by the late public disclosure that agents had used tear gas grenades even though that information had been given to , but overlooked by , Congress . Former Senator John Danforth led a ten month investigation into these questions and concluded that blame for the Waco bloodshed lies with the Davidians and not the FBI . Several groups , however , remain skeptical of the Danforth conclusions .
