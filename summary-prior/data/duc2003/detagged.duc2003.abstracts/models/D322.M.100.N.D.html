Several scams involving the sale of international art were uncovered between 1989 and 1994 . An Australian businessman was charged with fraud relating to the sale of Monet 's `` La Promenade '' . A Canadian was arrested after peddling 200 pieces of forged art . A San Diego man sold forged copies of paintings by Olaf Weighorst , a famous Western artist . Three San Francisco area men were arrested trying to sell $ 100 million worth of bogus Picassos and Rembrandts . Over 1600 pieces of bogus art were seized after a Los Angeles dealer tried to sell a fake Renoir to an undercover detective for $ 3.2 million .
