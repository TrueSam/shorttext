At least nineteen Los Angeles area residents died in fifteen regional automobile accidents reported during the period 01-12-89 to 11-18-90 . Eight of the accidents and eleven of the deaths were caused by cars crossing into an oncoming lane and striking another vehicle . The remaining eight casualities were the result of a car missing a curve or veering out of control off the highway . In eight of the incidents , authorities reportedly could not determine why the cars crossed over into oncoming lanes or otherwise were still investigating the causes . There were four non-casualty accidents whose cause could not be determined .
