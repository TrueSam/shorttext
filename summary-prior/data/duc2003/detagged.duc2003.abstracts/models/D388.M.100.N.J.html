Sludge is rich in nitrogen and phosphorous , important plant nutrients , and can reduce the need for water . Some say it will also reduce the need for harmful pesticides . It has , however , a major drawback - the odor . Nevertheless , 42 % of the 1.1m dry tons of sludge produced yearly in the UK are used for cereal crops and grassland . In 1990 , Los Angeles , for the first time , allowed sludge to be used on farmland . It could not be used on crops grown for human consumption , and the city and the farm concern were required to conduct extensive water and soil sampling .
