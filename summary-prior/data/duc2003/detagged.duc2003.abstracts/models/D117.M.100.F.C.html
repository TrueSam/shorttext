Homelessness is a major , long-standing and intractable problem in most cities , particularly San Francisco . Many homeless come from and continue a life of poverty , drug abuse , and physical or mental illness . Cities lack the resources , particularly jobs , shelters and public housing , to adequately address the problem ; and undercounting the homeless by census workers erroneously limits federal funding . Advocacy groups say criminalizing the homeless may derail constructive methods to deal with them . A study showed that the success rate is high for homeless families who are given subsidized housing . Meanwhile , many homeless are viciously attacked and murdered while living on the streets .
