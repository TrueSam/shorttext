Many believe driving while using a cell phone is dangerous , but there is no real evidence . Many jurisdictions have tried to enact laws regulating this activity but most have failed . In 2000 it was illegal for New York City cab drivers to use a cell phone while driving . Five small U.S. towns require hands-free speakerphones . Three states have minor regulations : in Massachusetts one hand must be on the wheel ; in Florida head sets cannot be used , one ear must be uncovered ; and in California safety directions must be in rental cars . Thirteen nations either ban driving and cell phone use or have requirements such as headsets or speakerphones .
