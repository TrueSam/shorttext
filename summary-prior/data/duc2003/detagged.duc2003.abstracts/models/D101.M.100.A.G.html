China has ordered its soldiers to `` fight to the death '' to save the dikes along the rapidly flooding Yangtze River . Some three million people are fighting floods along the Yangtze lower and middle embankments , while over 900,000 workers are in Northeast China . A ban on logging was announced the upper reaches of the Yangtze . Agriculture experts were mobilized to advise farmers on how to save farmlands . Provinces have been issued instructions to prepare emergency evacuation plans , and authorities have warned that contagious diseases could spread through the refugee camps . Authorities also predicted a high probability for higher floods next year .
