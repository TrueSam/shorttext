The Hubble space telescope , launched in 1990 , has been a remarkable scientific success , but several space repair missions were required to keep it functioning . The first was in 1993 , another in 1997 , and now in 1999 , still another is needed . The most recent problem was caused by gyroscope failure making its precision pointing system non-functioning . While the craft was in no peril , its inability to perform properly denied the scientific world much valuable data . NASA opted to send the shuttle Discovery on a repair mission in late 1999 , rather than in 2000 , as planned . That repair mission was successful and Hubble 's capabilities were upgraded .
