Bulimia and anorexia nervosa are eating disorders which most often afflict adolescent women , seldom men . Victims are characterized by an irrational compulsion to remain thin . Those suffering anorexia nervosa practice excessive dieting , sometimes to the point of starvation . Bulimics will binge eat , but then use laxatives or self-induced vomiting to keep their weight down . Causes are uncertain ; however , some experts believe they begin in childhood with parentally induced eating requirements . Some think anorexia nervosa is caused by diet-triggered opiates in the brain so that hunger is overridden by euphoria . Treatment for both is generally successful . Prozac may be prescribed for bulimics .
