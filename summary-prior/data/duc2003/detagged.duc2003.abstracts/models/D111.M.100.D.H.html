Over half the states considered limiting driver phone use , including Arizona , Connecticut , Georgia , Massachusetts , Nebraska , NY , Pennsylvania and Texas . NY prohibits cab driver phoning . Massachusetts requires phoning drivers to keep one hand on the wheel . Florida bans headsets , requiring one uncovered ear . Brooklyn , OH ; Marlboro Township , NJ ; Athens , CO ; and Hilltown , Consohocken , and Lebanon , PA ban or limit driver phone use . Over 300 municipalities considered local ordinances . Police , emergency vehicles , and dispatch users are exempt from bans , also drivers reporting dangers . Some ban support followed accidents caused by phone users . Brazil , Chile , England , France , Germany , Israel , Italy , Japan , Portugal , Singapore , Spain , Sweden , Switzerland , and parts of Australia ban or restrict driver phone use .
