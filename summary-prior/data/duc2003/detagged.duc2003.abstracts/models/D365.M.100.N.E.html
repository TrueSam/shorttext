The El Nino is responsible for rising temperatures in the eastern and central Pacific , drought in the western Pacific , dryness in Brazil , and increased precipitation in the Great Basin of the U.S. The El Nino also disrupts the jet stream which keeps extremely cold air far to the north and moderating temperatures in the Midwest . The El Nino in 1972 and 1973 destroyed the Peruvian fish meal catch while the El Nino in 1982 and 1983 caused a drought in Africa which drove up cocoa prices . The 1982-1983 El Nino also was blamed for 1,500 deaths and billions of dollars of damage around the world .
