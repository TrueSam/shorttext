Press reported Clinton 's relationship with Monica Lewinsky in January 1998 . Prosecutors investigated . Independent Counsel Kenneth Starr intimidated Lewinsky and gave her full immunity in exchange for testifying to an affair for Paula Jones ' sexual harassment lawsuit . The Republican-dominated House Judiciary Committee allowed broadcast of Clinton testimony with Starr repeatedly requesting details of sexual activities , and publication of Lewinsky 's written account . Republicans said Americans had the right to judge for themselves . Democrats said Republicans wanted to embarrass Clinton and demolish his presidency . At impeachment proceedings for perjury and obstructing justice , Senators divided along party lines . Republicans refused to declare guilt without loss of office . No one expected enough votes to remove Clinton .
