The sixteen NATO members in 1997generally favored the bids of former Soviet satellites to become NATO members . A consensus favored the Czech Republic , Poland and Hungary for acceptance in `` a first wave '' . Britain , France and Canada issued statements specifically supporting the Czechs . Britain did say the Czech army did not meet NATO standards . Russia was opposed to any eastward expansion of NATO , citing nuclear weapons closer to their border . Russia threatened economic reprisals against the Czechs . In July 1997 NATO invited Czech membership . Member nations ratified the invitation . The Czech Republic formally joined NATO in March 1999 .
