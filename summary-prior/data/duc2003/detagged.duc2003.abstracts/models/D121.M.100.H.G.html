Interpol has been urging at its international conferences a new strategy to combat worldwide cross-border crime , primarily because of increasingly sophisticated crime syndicates . The focus is on car thefts , smuggling , insurance fraud , child pornography , cross-border pollution , drug trafficking , and terrorism . Increased cooperation is necessary in order to gather evidence for prosecution and overcome obstacles in handing over terrorists . China has made concerted efforts to strengthen cooperation against organized crime , drugs , and terrorism especially with Hong Kong after the latter 's transition . Interpol is also working with a US firm in developing a worldwide network system for early warning against cyber attacks .
