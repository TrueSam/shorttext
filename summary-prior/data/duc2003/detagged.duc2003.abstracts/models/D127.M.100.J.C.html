The European Economic Community ( EEC ) was established in 1957 . In 1970 a fully integrated European Economic and Monetary Union ( EMU ) was proposed , which lead to a 1995 proposal for a single European currency , the Euro . European Union ( EU ) countries first linked their current systems to the new future single currency through a new European exchange rate mechanism . EU then determined which EU nations met established economic standards for conversion . 11 of the 15 EU nations were approved , 2 opted out and 2 were postponed as unqualified . Those 11 converted to the Euro in January 1999 , with July 2002 the scheduled close-out of their old currency systems .
