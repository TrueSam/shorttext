Tom Welch , former president of the SLOC , and his deputy , Dave Johnson , pleaded not guilty to federal charges that they conspired to bribe members of the IOC . Since the scandal broke in 1998 , several IOC members have been accused of wrongdoing . Australian IOC member Phil Coles was entertained by the SLOC at a cost of $ 60,000 . The IOC member from the Republic of Congo and the Chilean IOC member reportedly accepted cash payments of $ 10,000 and $ 50,000 , respectively . Additionally , the Ecuadorian IOC member accepted travel money and a `` scholarship '' for his middle-age stepdaughter who was not in school at the time .
