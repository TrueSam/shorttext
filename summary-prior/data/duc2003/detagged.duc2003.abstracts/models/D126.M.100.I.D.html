A new Canadian Territory was created on 1 April 1999 . The eastern portion of the Northwest Territories was split off from the Northwest Territories to form Nunavut . Inuits make up 85 % of the population . It is one of the most remote and sparsely-settled-regions of the world . Only 25,000 people live in its 77,000 square miles . Snow , ice and tundra cover the Territory . No roads connect the 26 isolated settlement . Alcoholism , suicide and violent crime levels are high . Housing is overcrowded . The Inuits are hardy people , and the new Inuit government of Nunavut hopes to rally them to overcome the dire social and economic conditions .
