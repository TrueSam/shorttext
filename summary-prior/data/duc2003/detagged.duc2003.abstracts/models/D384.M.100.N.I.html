Planning and work has been underway for several years to establish a station in space to be a base for eventual colonization of the moon . The Russian MIR-2 was the starting point , then , U.S. technology with its laboratory module was added . Plans were for an eventual international space station , involving the U.S. , Russia , Canada , Japan , and the European Space Association . Impediments to plans included building a launch vehicle with a 200-to 300-ton lift capability . Research into life support systems , especially supplying oxygen , water and food , was underway , as were experiments on counteracting the effects of prolonged weightlessness and blocking radiation .
