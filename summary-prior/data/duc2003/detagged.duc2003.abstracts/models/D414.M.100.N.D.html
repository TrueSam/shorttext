In 1991 Cuba agreed to export four million tons of sugar to the Soviet Union . Cuba renegotiated with the ex-Soviet states in 1992 , with Russia taking up to a million tons , and Kazakhstan and Latvia lesser amounts . The Cuban sugar harvest declined precipitously after 1991-92 , and in 1993 the total export revenue , mostly from sugar , was only $ 1.7 billion . In 1994Cuba could not fulfill is commitment to export 1.5 million tons of sugar to Russia in exchange for four million tons of oil . Cuba exported sugar to Italy in return for semi-finished food products , and to China for food and medicine .
