The Year 2000 ( Y2K ) problem , where computers are expected to to recognize the date change from 99 to 00 , have been known about since the mid-1980s . Even so , active measures to deal with the problem and make computers compliant did not begin until 1996 . By 1998 , measures to deal with the Y2K problem were underway at the international level , such as the Declaration of Cooperation and Support of Asian Y2K , to cooperation on the local level . Despite this cooperation , extremist groups were urging members to take measures to protect themselves . There is some belief that fear of Y2K will present the greatest problems .
