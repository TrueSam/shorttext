Though a statistical rating system is used to determine who plays in the national championship game , crowning of the champion is still subject to controversy . The national title will still be determined by postseason polls . If neither team in the championship game plays well , it may go to a third-rated team that was impressive in its bowl game . The rating system could also determine that a team ranked third in the polls would play in the championship game . Further , the championship game could fail to draw a big television audience if both teams are from the same region of the country .
