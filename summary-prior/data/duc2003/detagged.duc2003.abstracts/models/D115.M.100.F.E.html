The Supreme Court , in a controversial 5 to 4 vote , upheld a Federal court decision to ban the use of statistical sampling in the 200 census . Favored by the Clinton Administration , proponents maintain that statistical sampling , along with traditional methods , will allow for a more accurate count , especially of minority populations . Over 8 million people , mostly minorities , were not counted in the 1990 census . House Republicans , whose suit led to the Supreme Court decision , maintain that statistical sampling will be used to the benefit of Democrats since count results could be used to redraw voting district lines .
