Iran and Iraq are meeting to improve relations . Conciliatorily , Iraq unilaterally withdrew from conquered Irani territory , released Irani prisoners , turned over Moujahedeen and offered to ship oil to Iran . Their meetings seek to : achieve a phased settlement to the dispute over the Shatt al- ' Arab waterway ; prevent the establishment of a semi-independent Kurdish state in north Iraq ; with other countries , establish a joint policy against Washington ; work toward easing the effects of UN sanctions against Iraq , without violating Security Council resolutions ; and return to relations at the ambassadorial level . To date , the Irani held Iraqi Gulf War aircraft have not been returned .
