There are a number of fatal accidents on California highways for which there appear to be no apparent cause . Most of these accidents involve two or more vehicles and are caused by an automobile swerving into oncoming traffic or by improper lance changes . In the case of single car fatalities , the drivers have lost control of their car and run off the road . Speed does not appear to be the primary cause in the multiple car accidents since many of the drivers were driving at or below the speed limit . Speed , however , did appear to be a factor in the single car accidents .
