Efforts at raising plants by hydroponic farming are showing success . A Utah State experiment simulating lunar or Martian conditions through variations in lighting and atmosphere , has grown a variety of wheat in hydroponic gardens . There is a successful private hydroponic project growing tomatoes in watered ceramic pebbles . A modern factory grows chicory in the darkness of a computer-controlled hydroponic shed . Drug agents have discovered marijuana plants in high-tech glasshouses equipped with controlled artificial lighting and nutrient-enriched solutions . One system used wooden framing , plastic tubing to carry water , reflective sheets to hold light in , and carbon dioxide to mature the plants .
