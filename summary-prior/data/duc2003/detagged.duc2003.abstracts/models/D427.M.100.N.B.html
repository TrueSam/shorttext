Damage to the eyes from exposure to ultraviolet light is cumulative , and includes such maladies as eye cancer , age-related cataracts , age-related macular degeneration , ageing and cancer of the circumocular skin , pterygium , and photokeratitis . The ozone hole above the Antarctic allows increased levels of ultraviolet rays to reach the earth . To restore the vision of an eye with a cataract , the cloudy lens must be removed and may be replaced by a plastic implant . Proton treatment has been used successfully to treat melanoma of the eye . Sunglasses with lenses of good optical quality are essential eye protection .
