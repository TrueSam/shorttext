Rabies cases of serious dimension have occurred in China , Mexico , the southwestern US , South America , Serbia , and Laos . Several deaths have been caused by rabies . In the Death Valley area of California , persons were warned of possible rabid foxes , coyotes , bats , and any meat-eating animals . In many parts of the world , inoculation of pets and livestock has been stepped up . Some jurisdictions have ordered pets to be kept indoors , and some have imposed quarantines . In cases wherein infected animals have been identified , they have been destroyed . In the US , 1993 had the most rabies cases in more than three decades .
