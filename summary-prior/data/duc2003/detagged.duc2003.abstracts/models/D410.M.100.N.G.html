Nine European Community ( EC ) countries - Germany , France , the Netherlands , Belgium , Luxembourg , Italy , Spain , Portugal and Greece - have signed the Schengen Accord , designed to abolish formal controls on passports and goods so that travelers can pass freely across internal West European borders . Internal checks will be abolished when external frontiers have been sufficiently strengthened . Britain , Ireland and Denmark are withholding full commitment until that strengthening and certain non-EC matters are confirmed . Participating countries will be obligated to adopt their national information systems to a central information system in Strasbourg , especially in relation to fighting drug trafficking and linking police and immigration authorities .
