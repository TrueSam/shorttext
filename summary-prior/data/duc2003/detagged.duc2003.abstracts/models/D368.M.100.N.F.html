In vitro fertilization is the process whereby a woman 's egg is fertilized by a man 's sperm in a lab dish . The fertilized egg can then be implanted into the womb of an infertile woman . This procedure has allowed couples who were unable to conceive , to have a child . A donor female 's egg is used in the process . Sometimes fertilized eggs are frozen for later use . In England , there is a shortage of donor eggs . A suggestion has been made that ovaries from aborted female fetuses be used since they may contain up to a million eggs ; an adult fertile woman 's ovaries might contain 100,000 eggs . Such a plan , however , is highly controversial .
