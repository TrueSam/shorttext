In a series of agreements , Cuba has exported about 12.7m tons of sugar to the Soviet Union in exchange for at least 12.5m tons of crude oil ; and as much as 6.5m tons to Russia itself for 23m barrels of at least 8 tons of oil . In 1993 , Cuban sugar was one of several products sent to China on a $ 400 million trade exchange . Cuba has also exported 740,000 tons of sugar to China , 200,000 tons to Kazakhstan , 50,000 tons to Latvia , 400,000 tons to Iran and South Korea , 14,000 tons to Morocco , and 100,000 tons to Indonesia .
