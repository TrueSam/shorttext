New research studies are providing valuable insight into the probable causes of schizophrenia . Danish researchers have shown that family history as well as place ( urban areas ) and season of birth ( esp . February or March ) are the best predictors of the likelihood of schizophrenia . Other research indicates a defect in the body 's olfactory system -- probably a hereditary predisposition -- may contribute to schizophrenia . There is also strong evidence that gene abnormalities on Chromosome 1 may cause schizophrenia . Also evidenced is impairment of the brain 's cognitive function area and deficiencies in the thalamus . Overall , research strongly links the development of schizophrenia to genetics .
