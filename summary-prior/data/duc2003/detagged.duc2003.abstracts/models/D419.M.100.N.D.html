There is an ongoing search for environmentally correct and profitable methods of getting rid of the hundreds of millions of tires that are discarded every year . Malaysia sunk a million tires off its coast to make artificial reefs that attract fish . A British firm shreds three million tires per year into `` crumb '' for use in making carpet underlay . `` Crumb '' is also used in road construction . A United States firm operates a facility in Britain that burns old tires to produce electricity while emitting virtually no harmful gases . Scrap tires are used in making jogging tacks , crash barriers , jungle gyms , mud flaps , sandals , doormats and hockey pucks .
