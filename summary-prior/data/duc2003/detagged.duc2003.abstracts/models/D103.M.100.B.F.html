Online auction houses are among the fastest growing Internet businesses . Leader of the group is eBay . It continues to increase its profits , expand its coverage , and even merge with or acquire competitors . Internet auctioning is susceptible to fraud primarily through false claims for merchandise offered or non-delivery after purchase . It is difficult to tag the auction houses with fraud unless they can be shown to have knowledge of a product offered . ebay , however , is under investigation for fraud by New York and Pennsylvania consumer authorities . Many sellers have come to rely on Internet auctions so any downtime causes them to lose business .
