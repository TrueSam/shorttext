The sun 's ultraviolet ( UV ) rays are more damaging to the eyes than other portions of the solar spectrum . Long-term exposure to UV contributes to ocular and circumocular cancer , conjunctival changes , and to age-related cataracts and macular degeneration . Wearing sunglasses made to protect against UV is essential in guarding against destructive rays . A hole in the ozone above southern Chile caused an increase in eyes problems . Protecting the ozone layer that filters out the UV will decrease the volume of eye-threatening rays that reach the earth . New surgical procedures and proton therapy are being used to alleviate UV-caused eye disorders .
