The Waco disaster occurred on April 19 , 1993 after a 51 day siege by government agents of the religious sect Branch Dividian 's compound resulting in the deaths of 80 Davidians . An initial Justice Department report claimed all deaths were the fault of the Dividians . Six years later , responding to accusations of government cover-up , lawsuits and new FBI revelations , Attorney General Janet Reno appointed an independent investigator to learn the truth . The results of former Senator Danforth 's investigation was that federal agents did not start the fire or shoot at cult members causing death , and did not engage in a major cover-up .
