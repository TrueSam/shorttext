Phosphate and nitrogen-rich sewage sludge improves soil 's water retention and crop yields , and reduces need for harmful pesticides and expensive commercial fertilizer . But sludge smells and could contain heavy metals and disease-causing bacteria . US sludge fertilizes crops not consumed by humans . UK sludge fertilizes cereal and industrial crops . UK sludge use is expected to rise because of EU regulations on treatment and a ban on dumping at sea . Dehydrated sludge is composted with other organic waste . Beneficial organic soil additions include ash , vegetable waste , chicken feathers , manure , seaweed extract , and carbon-rich newsprint . Farmyard manure and compost fertilize Russian potatoes and vegetables . Chinese organic fertilizer use is decreasing .
