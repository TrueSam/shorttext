The Supreme Court ruled over a decade ago that teaching creationism violated the constitutional separation of church and state . Opponents of this ruling now pursue their only remaining tactic -- to limit the teaching of evolution . This tactic was recently , effectively employed in Kansas . There a conservative Board of Education muddied the science curriculum by ruling that the teaching of evolution is optional in each or the state 's 304 school districts . Furthermore , questions on the `` big bang '' theory of creation , age of the earth or universe , and macroevolution , i.e. , the process of change from one species to another , would not be on any state standardized tests .
