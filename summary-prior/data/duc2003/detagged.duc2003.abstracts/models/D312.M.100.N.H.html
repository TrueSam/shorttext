Hydroponically raised plants grow in nutrient-rich solutions rather than soil , such as tomatoes in ceramic pebbles or water , or chicory on shelves with nutrient-bearing waters pumped through them . Researchers simulated lunar and Martian light and atmosphere conditions and grew Mexican dwarf wheat sufficient to feed one person indefinitely from 13 square yards . Marijuana has been grown illegally in basements , garages , and greenhouses using 1,000-watt grow lights and automated irrigation systems . Plastic tubing carries water to plants . Reflective sheets hold light and carbon dioxide in to mature plants at a low height . Controlled amount and angle of artificial light simulates changing seasons so plants are harvested year-round .
