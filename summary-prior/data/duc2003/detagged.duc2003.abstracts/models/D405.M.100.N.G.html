Recent detections of celestial phenomena reveal notably a string of galaxies 200 million light years away . Named the Great Wall , it is the largest stucture seen so far in the universe . Also detected were gigantic ripples of matter formed 15 billiion years ago from the Big Bang . Additionally , there are giant `` blobs of material '' in the Milky Way which seem to evidence the existence of black holes . Other discoveries are : a Supernova , the brightest in 400 years , with a rapidly spinning neutron star ( pulsar ) . and electron neutrinos ; individual cephoid variable stars ; a neutron star destroying a companion sun ; and birth of a pair of quasars .
