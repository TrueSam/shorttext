Before the 1998 football season the NCAA Division 1-A championship was decided by a media and a coaches ' poll . Sometimes they disagreed . The BCS was established to ensure the two best teams competed at season 's end . Six major conferences and four traditional bowls participate . The BCS standings are based on poll rankings , computer surveys , schedule strength and team records . Champions of the six conferences receive automatic bids . At-large bids are open to teams with at least nine wins , and a rating of 12th or higher in the BCS standings . The top two teams play for the championship . The championship venue rotates among the four bowls .
