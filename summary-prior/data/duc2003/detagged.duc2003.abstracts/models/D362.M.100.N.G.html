In 1988 , Federal Agents apprehended 13,200 smugglers and 28,108 smuggled immigrants . In the following two years , over 500 illegal aliens and at least seven smugglers from Mexico and Central America were apprehended in raids on `` drop houses '' in Southern California and on airplanes bound from Los Angeles to the East Coast . Some aliens paid as much as $ 4,000 for delivery and placement . The U.S. Coast Guard detained over 1600 illegal Chinese iimmigrants . Hundreds of illegal Chinese immigrants were recovered from boats sneaking into coastal harbors in the U.S. and Japan . Authorities also destroyed a smuggling ring bringing Chinese across the Canadian border .
