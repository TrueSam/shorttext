Neutrinos are tiny particles having no electrical charge . Only recently we learned they have mass . There are three known types of neutrinos . Nobelist Frederick Reines isolated the electron neutrino in 1956 . The muon neutrino was identified in 1962 . Detection of the tau neutrino was even more difficult . Detection efforts were carried out under Antarctica , under the Aegean Sea , under the Alps , and in other locations . The super-Kamiokande effort in Japan was most successful . An international team found evidence that neutrinos have mass , and suggested the presence of tau neutrinos . The tau was positively identified at the Fermi laboratory in July 2000 .
