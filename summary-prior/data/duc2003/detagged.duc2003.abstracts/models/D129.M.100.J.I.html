Everybody prepared for the moment that 1999 became 2000 . Post-1997 equipment and the Apple Macintosh were compliant , but older equipment and software needed repairs or replacement . National and international commissions were established . Many websites were created . State , community , school , and church entities held meetings and issued publications . Financial support and a free exchange of information were encouraged . Survivalists urged people to buy generators , store food and water , and learn gardening and canning techniques . Lawyers anticipated a wave of litigation , sought legal protections , and counseled full disclosure . Special areas of concern were the economy , transportation , utilities , stock exchanges , and health care .
