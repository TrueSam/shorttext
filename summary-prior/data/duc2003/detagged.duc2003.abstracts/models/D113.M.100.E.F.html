President Clinton 's denial , but later admission , about having an affair with intern Monica Lewinsky , led to his impeachment by the House on charges of perjury and obstruction of justice . At first , Lewinsky also denied , under oath , of having a sexual affair with the President . Later , the Office of the Special Prosecutor investigating Clinton granted her immunity to testify before the grand jury . A videotape of that testimony was played at the senate 's trial of Clinton but she was not called to testify in person . Lewinsky later went public with the story of her affair with the President in a two-hour interview with Barbara Walters on ABC TV .
