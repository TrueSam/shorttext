We are reentering a social climate in which the cigar is tolerated . This is evidenced by the emergence of more , young cigar smokers , the growth in production and sales of cigars , particularly the high end ( i.e. , expensive ) cigars , the proliferation of designated cigar smoking areas in quality hotels and restaurants and the promotion of cigar smoking events , such as dinners in those very same hotels and restaurants . Cigar smoking is no longer only for `` old men '' . In Boston , a group of women have their own little cigar club at the Ritz-Carlton there , and one of the members is Anwar Sadat 's daughter .
