GM recalls stemmed from sticking throttles , wiring problems , fuel leaks , faulty seat mechanisms , faulty coolants , shoulder belt problems , and an emissions control problem . Ford recalls stemmed from defective seats , handbrake levers , air bags , fuel tank seals , throttle position sensors , and fuel injector parts ; and various emission system problems . Chrysler recalled cars with rusty suspension parts , and to replace fuel injectors to reduce carbon monoxide emissions . Other recalls were by Toyota for problem seat belts , Subaru for rear suspension corrosion , Nissan for defective power-steering hoses , Rover for a seat belt problem , Mazda for defective air bags and ignition switches , Hyundai to retune emissions systems , and Volkswagen to correct a cooling system .
