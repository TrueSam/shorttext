Disasters can occur in transportation tunnels . In the United States , Switzerland and the United Kingdom , 245 people were injured because of fires in railroad tunnels . Construction and rescue workers were threatened when a fire in a southern California subway tunnel , which was under construction , caused a 150-foot section of the roof to collapse . A seven-vehicle pile-up occurred in a tunnel on the Golden State Freeway in California . Most disastrous was the deaths of 1,462 Islamic pilgrims in a stampede , which occurred in a pedestrian tunnel in Mecca , Saudi Arabia , during the Hajj in 1990 .
