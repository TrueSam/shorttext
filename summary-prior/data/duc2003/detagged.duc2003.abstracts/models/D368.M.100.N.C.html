In vitro fertilization involves having a woman 's egg fertilized by sperm in a laboratory , and then within days , implanting it into the mother or ' surrogate ' , or freezing it for later implantation . Techniques change ; moral , scientific and legal issues abound . The newest technique involves removing the ovaries from an aborted fetus , ' maturing ' the eggs in the laboratory , fertilizing them with sperm , and implanting the resultant embryo into the womb . Doctors long believed that the only selectable IVF characteristic was skin color , but London scientists now can identify sex in pre-implanted embryos . In American , 192 IVF programs offer a range of assisted reproductive technology ( ART ) services .
