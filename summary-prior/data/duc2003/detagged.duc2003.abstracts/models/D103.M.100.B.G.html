Hundreds of Web auction sites are online now and are particularly successful in selling used goods . A site called Internet Auction List ( www.internetauctionlist.com ) can give you an idea of exactly how many . The mother of them all is Ebay . It has hosted some 43 million auctions since it was founded in 1995 and is raising $ 1.11 billion for international expansion in the selling of a huge range of goods . It has merged recently with Autotrader.com to increase lucrative used auto sales and with Butterfield to expand its auction of higher-end items . Other fast-growing auctioneers are Amazon.com , Yahoo.com , Onsale Inc. and Auction Atlanta .
