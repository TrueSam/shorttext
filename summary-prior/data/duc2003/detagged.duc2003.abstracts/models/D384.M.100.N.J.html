Experts predict that space development will shift from satellites with position dependent missions , such as communications satellites , to satellite base stations . These space stations will be facilities for research on the long-term effects of low gravity on animals and plants and , eventually , will be staging areas for supplies ferried from Earth to lunar colonies . NASA intends for its space station Freedom to be operational by 1999 and an outpost on the Moon active by 2005 . Additionally , the U.S. and Russia agreed to jointly develop space station Alpha which will become an international project with Canada , Japan , and the European Space Agency participating .
