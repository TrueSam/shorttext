The damming of the Yangtze River has begun . The damming is part of the Three Gorges Dam Construction Project on the middle of the Yangtze river , China 's longest river . The project , the largest of its kind , has involved engineering experts from around the world as well as extensive foreign investment . German banks , for example , have given China $ 270 million in loans . In planning for the dam , China has begun the relocation of over one-million people . In addition , a forest preserve has been established near the dam to help preserve the over one-thousand plant species unique to the region .
