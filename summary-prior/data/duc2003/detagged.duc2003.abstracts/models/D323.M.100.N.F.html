Complaints of authors borrowing from the works of others have been alleged since at least the time of Shakespeare . In modern times , allegations of plagiarism have often led to strong denials and lawsuits . US senator Joe Biden and civil rights leader Martin Luther King are among the notables who borrowed from the works of others . Humorist Art Buchwald brought a multimillion dollar lawsuit against a movie company alleging the use of a story of his as basis for a movie . The Wall Street Journal fired an assistant editor because of a charge of plagiarism , which was strongly denied by the editor .
