In vitro fertilization involved fertilizing a woman 's egg with a man 's sperm in the laboratory . The egg is then incubated for up to 36 hours before it is implanted in the woman . The success rate for such a procedure is around 12 % . The odds for a successful pregnancy appear to increase if the embryo is frozen before implantation and a woman can have several eggs fertilized at the same time . Doctors warn that if a woman has not had a successful pregnancy by the sixth attempt , it is unlikely the procedure will be successful and they counsel against further attempts .
