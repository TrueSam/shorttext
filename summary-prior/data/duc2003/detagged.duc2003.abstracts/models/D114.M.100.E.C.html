After NATO made its policy decision to expand eastward , the Czech Republic received early and strong support for membership from Canada and France among others . On July 8 , 1997 , the Czech Republic , along with Poland and Hungary , became the first new nations invited to join . Russian had expressed to the Czech Republic its opposition to NATO enlargement , fearing it would threaten its security . After negotiations , protocol signing , and being ratified by all sixteen current members , the Czech Republic , Poland and Hungary formally joined NATO on March 12 , 1999 . The Czech army would need considerable time to come to NATO standards .
