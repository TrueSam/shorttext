Many automobile accidents are recorded in which the cause can not be explained . After mechanical failure , bad weather conditions , and poor visibility are ruled out as causes , questions still remain . The types of accidents that fall into this category occur when a car misses a curve or swerves suddenly or crosses the highway median into oncoming traffic . Alcohol has also been ruled out as a cause in most of these accidents . One might suspect drowsiness or distraction but , unfortunately , these cannot always be checked since many of the accidents result in fatalities .
