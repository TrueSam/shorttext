The awarding of the Nobel Prize to Doctors Without Borders , also know by their French title Medecins Sans Frontieres , met with little criticism . Even so , the humanitarian organization founded in 1971 by 10 French doctors has not been without its detractors . The organization was founded in protest to the neutrality of the Red Cross . Doctors Without Borders will openly criticize conditions and government policies in countries where they are working . This practiced has led them to withdraw from Kosovo to protest government instituted terror , for example . They also withdrew from North Korea and Madagascar following disputers with those governments .
