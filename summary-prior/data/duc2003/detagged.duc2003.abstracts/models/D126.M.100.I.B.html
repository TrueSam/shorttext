Nunavut encompasses 770,000 square miles of tundra . No roads lead to Nunavut . The only connections to the outside world are by air and supply ferries that operate during the few months of semi-thaw . The most common form of transport is the snowmobile . Unemployment is 30 percent , but in some villages nearly half the inhabitants subsist on government doles . The suicide rate is six times the national average . Alcoholism is epidemic , and solvent sniffing is off the charts . The birth rate is similar to that of developing countries . Nunavut has Canada 's highest rate of violent crime , with one attack reported each year for every 19 residents .
