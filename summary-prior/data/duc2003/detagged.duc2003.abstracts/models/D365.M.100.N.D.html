El Nino is a cyclical weather pattern that develops near the Equator in the Pacific Ocean . It can disrupt existing weather patterns and cause catastrophic conditions . The 1982-83 El Nino was blamed for 1500 deaths , and world-wide damage in the billions of dollars . It worsened the drought in Africa , and caused severe storms in California . Those storms destroyed the nesting areas of species of rare birds . The Peruvian fishmeal industry was ravaged by the 1972-73 El Nino The 1992 floods in Peru , Bolivia and Ecuador were blamed in part on El Nino . It may be that El Nino is one cause of increased global warning .
