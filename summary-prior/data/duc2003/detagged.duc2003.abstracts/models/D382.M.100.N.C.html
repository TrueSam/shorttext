Among automobile manufacturer 's efforts to develop low-polluting , hydrogen-powered vehicles , three differing technologies compete . The first is to use hydrogen gas directly as a fuel , i.e. , burn it directly in the engine 's combustion chamber similarly to the present-day use of gasoline . A second method is to use hydrogen as an energy source within a fuel cell . The fuel cell converts the hydrogen directly to electricity by electromechanical means , which then powers the vehicle . The third method uses a nickel-based and hydrogen-occluded alloy ( nickel-hydrogen ) battery . Under this method , the vehicle gets its power from electricity stored in the battery , which must be frequently recharged .
