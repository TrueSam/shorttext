Rabies is wide spread and involves dogs , cats , and farm animals as well as wild life , including foxes and bats . Measures being taken to prevent the spread of rabies include vaccination of pets and live stock and leaving vaccinated bait for wild animals . In Shanghai , all pet dogs were ordered killed because of an outbreak of rabies while in Central and South America , ways are being sought to reduce the bat population . Other than vaccination , killing the infected animals is the most widespread remedy . People also are being warned to avoid contact with wild animals and are being urged to keep their pets indoors .
