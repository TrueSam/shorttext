Cuba 's state tobacco company is increasing its output of cigars and the Dominican Republic has increased its export of cigars . Why ? Because cigar smoking is increasing in popularity among younger and older men . A Los Angeles cigar store reports business up 30 % from last year . The Ritz Carlton at Dana Point recently dedicated a cigar-smoking room . A hotel in Newport Beach has a dining room for cigar smokers . And a West Hollywood hotel features a monthly `` Cigar Smokers Night . '' But it is not just for Californian men . In the Boston Ritz Carlton a group of women have formed their own little cigar club .
