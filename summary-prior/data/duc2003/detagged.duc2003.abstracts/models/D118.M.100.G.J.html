High-level U.S. officials discussed with African and European officials the creation of a standby force of African troops to deal with tribal warfare . The U.S. also denounced the tribal violence in Kenya as being caused by the government 's `` strong-arm tactics '' and dispatched envoy Jesse Jackson to Nairobi to urge the Kenyan president to take steps to end the conflict . African leaders have also acted to end the violence . The former Tanzanian president created a foundation to promote African unity , and a South African commission is investigating the causes of violence , but has thus far failed to find the causes of black-on-black violence .
