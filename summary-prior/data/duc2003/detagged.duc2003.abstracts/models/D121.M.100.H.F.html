Interpol , the International Police Organization headquartered in Lyon , France , generally takes the lead in organizing multinational efforts to combat crime . Because of the increased sophistication of criminals , Interpol is constantly faced with the need to upgrade its strategies and increase the level of international cooperation . Car theft and smuggling is a growing problem for police units world-wide . Countering the drug traffic , long a problem , is worsened by the growing strength of the drug lords . In addition to fighting crime across national borders , police units must now form stronger links to combat international terrorism .
