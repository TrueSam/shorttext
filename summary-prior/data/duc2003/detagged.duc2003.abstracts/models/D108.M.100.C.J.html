Traditionally , Iowa 's precinct caucuses are the first Presidential delegate selection event , followed by the New Hampshire primary eight days later . In 1999 Louisiana and South Carolina both announced their primaries would be earlier in the year , forcing Iowa to hold its caucuses on Jan. 24 , 2000 and New Hampshire to hold its first-in-the nation primary on Feb. 1 . Additionally , politicos in New York and California recommended changes in their state 's delegate selection processes . Finally , the Supreme Court in June 2000 rejected California 's primary election system , forcing it , Washington State , and Alaska to change the way they conduct future primaries .
