E. coli is estimated to kill 60 , and sicken 73,000 Americans each year . The strain 0157 : H7 , discover and named in 1982 , is especially prevalent . It lives in cow and human feces and is spread by undercooked and improperly butchered beef , unpasturized juice , unwashed ground crops , tainted water , and poor personal hygiene . The worst outbreaks were in Washington State in 1993 and New York State in 1999 . Other states hit include Georgia , Maine , Ohio , and Texas . Scotland , Japan and Canada had deaths and there have been outbreaks in Australia , Africa , and Europe . This bacterium has become a worldwide problem .
