As the year 2000 ( Y2K ) approached there was worldwide concern over ramifications of computer problems associated with a four-digit change of year . Some seemed overconfident that all was well while others forecast disaster . Some organizations were `` Y2K compliant '' as early as 1998 , but smaller ones tended to lag behind . Groups were formed and businesses were established to deal with Y2K technical problems or prepare for major economic and social disruption . Airlines in Poland and Viet Nam canceled all flights for Jan.1 , 2000 , but in the U.S. prominent officials announced plans to be in the air as 2000 arrived . Actually nobody knew what would happen .
