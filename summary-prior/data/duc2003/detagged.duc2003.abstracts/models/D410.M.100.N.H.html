The Schengen Accord , signed in 1990 by France , Germany , the Netherlands , Belgium , and Luxembourg , would create barrier-free internal markets and speed cross-border traffic between member countries . It would abolish internal passport checks , strengthen external checks , remove traffic uobstacles , unify visa procedures , and link immigration and police authorities through a central computer system . Airports would separate international and EC flight arrivals . Implementation date moved from January 1993 to March [1995 -RSB- . Germany , France , Italy , Spain , Portugal , Greece and the Benelux countries -- all the EC countries except Denmark , Ireland , and the UK ( which opposed lifting controls on people ) -- signed the agreement . Austria had observer status . Finland and Sweden kept their distance .
