Cashews are a major crop for Tanzania , Mozambique , and Vietnam and are under development in India and Sri Lanka . Over the past decade , the cashew harvest in these countries has been adversely affected by drought , cold , weather , and social upheaval . The decline in the cashew harvest affected not only the farmer but also the workers in the nut processing facilities as plants operated below capacity . There has been a concerted effort to turn production around and harvest were up in most countries . Labor problems in Mozambique threaten this progress as production workers seek to bar the export of raw cashews ; a move that means lower prices for farmers .
