`` Medecines Sans Frontierres '' was founded as an humanitarian aid organization in 1971 by ten French physicians who were disillusioned with the International Red Cross . `` Doctors Without Borders '' now has over 2000 volunteer medical personnel serving in over 70 countries . Its operating principle is that all victims of disaster , whether of natural or human origin , have a right to receive professional assistance as quickly as possible without regard to national boundaries and political circumstances or sympathies . In addition to providing these services , a second mission , is to speak out against the atrocities it witnesses in the hope of preventing their future occurrence .
