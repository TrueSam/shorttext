References to stoicism often are found in contemporary artistic productions . In `` The Story of Philosophy , '' Durant relates an incident from the life of Epictetus as a classic example of stoicism . Burrows ' `` Stoics '' reveals a dance language markedly original in exposing feeling . Elovich 's play , `` If Men Could Talk , the Stories They Would Tell , '' is a plea for men to break out of the emotional ghetto of masculine stoicism . Bunuel 's comedy , `` The Discreet Charm Of the Bourgeoisie , '' presents a funny and winning look at stoicism . In everyday life , people refer to stoicism when discussing the Victorian era , hardship from economic recession , etc .
