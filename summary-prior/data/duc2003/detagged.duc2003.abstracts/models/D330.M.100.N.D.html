By September 1990 Iraq had withdrawn from 100 square miles of Iranian territory held since the August 1988 cease-fire . Some prisoners had already been exchanged . The two countries were talking about an agreement on oil policy . When talks resumed after the Gulf War , Iraq 's main objective was the speedy return of the planes that had been flown to Iran . Iran wanted Iraq to return members of an Iranian resistance group , Moujahedeen , in exchange for food and medicine . Other items discussed were : use of the Shatt al- ' Arab waterway ; the prevention of the establishment of a semi-independent Kurdish state ; and joint economic cooperation .
