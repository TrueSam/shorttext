Fires have been the most frequent cause of transportation tunnel disasters although collisions and collapses have also occurred . Los Angeles has suffered serious delays in Metro Rail subway construction because of fires . A train collision in the Severn Railroad tunnel in Great Britain injured 185 and a fire at the channel tunnel terminus necessitated an evacuation . A New York subway fire near the Waldorf Astoria Hotel halted traffic . Fifty passengers were injured when a locomotive caught fire in a Zurich tunnel . The greatest loss of life in a tunnel incident occurred when over 1400 Muslim pilgrims suffocated or were trampled to death escaping a tunnel while en route to Mecca .
