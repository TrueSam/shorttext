Anorexia Nervosa is an eating disorder in which patients starve themselves . Bulimia is a related eating disorder in which those so afflicted ingest large quantities of food and then induce vomiting to stay thin . Both are seen primarily in young women who perceive themselves as already overweight or on their way there . An addiction to the opiates produced by the brain while dieting may cause eating disorders . One study shows that most anorexic girls outgrow the disorder as they grow older Cathy Rigby , gymnast turned actress , battled bulimia for 12 years . She binged on food and then took laxatives and induced vomiting .
