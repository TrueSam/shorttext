The March and April 2000 floods in Mozambique were devastating . One million were displaced and more that 400 killed . Scores of international relief organizations and 16 nations stepped up to help . The first assistance to the country focused on search and rescue operations and providing and distributing food , medical supplies , and water purification capabilities . Some of Mozambique 's debts were forgiven or payments postponed . Cholera was feared . Seeds were sent so a second crop could be planted to avoid massive famine . The flood 's displacement of landmines required technical assistance . Money was provided for a flood warning system and rebuilding the infrastructure .
