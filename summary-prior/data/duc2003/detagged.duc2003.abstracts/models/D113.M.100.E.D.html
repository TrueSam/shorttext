In January 1998 Vernon Jordan , a Clinton friend , said Lewinsky told him that she did not have a sexual relationship with Clinton . In August Lewinsky testified before a grand jury after being granted transactional immunity . She reportedly testified that she did have sexual encounters with Clinton , something she had denied in the Paula Jones trial . The Independent Prosecutor sent the testimony to the House . House prosecutors said Lewinsky 's testimony was credible , and asked the Senate to subpoena her for the Senate impeachment trial .
