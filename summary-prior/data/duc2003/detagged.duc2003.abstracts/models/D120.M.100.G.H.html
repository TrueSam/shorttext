The Three Gorges Project , involving damming the Yangtze and constructing coffer dams and ship locks , will combat flooding and generate 84.7 billion kwh of electricity annually . A central link in China 's power grid , it will promote economic growth , social development , and tourism , aided by a mobile phone network . The project will create $ 1.1 billion in western business and loans . 1.3 million people will be relocated as towns and 26,000 hectares of farmland are submerged . Provinces where migrants resettle will receive subsidies . 125,000 will be relocated so slopes can be planted to lessen soil erosion . Dalaoling State Forest Park is being created to preserve rare plants from the inundated area .
