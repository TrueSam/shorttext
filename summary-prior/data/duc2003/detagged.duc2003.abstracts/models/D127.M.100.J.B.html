Belgium , Germany , Spain , France , Ireland , Italy , Luxembourg , the Netherlands , Austria , Portugal , and Finland will become founders of the single currency known as the euro on January 1 , 1999 . The other four countries in the European Union will not be joining then . Britain and Denmark have opted out , while Sweden and Greece are not economically ready to join . Britain is fighting demands that their currency be linked to the euro through a new European exchange rate mechanism after the euro debuts . Britain argues that having a strict European inflation control system would prevent the devaluation of currencies outside the single currency .
